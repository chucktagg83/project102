Experience the Flavor of the South !!!

At Savory Haven, we invite you to experience the warmth and hospitality that Southern cuisine is known for. Each dish is crafted with love, bringing the flavors of the South to life. Whether you’re dining with family, friends, or a loved one, our menu promises a delicious journey that will leave you feeling right at home.Join us for a meal filled with laughter, good company, and the unforgettable taste of Southern comfort food!

Southern Comfort Cuisine

Start your meal with our selection of mouthwatering appetizers that set the tone for a true Southern feast. From crispy Fried Green Tomatoes served with a zesty remoulade to our Shrimp and Grits Bites, each dish is crafted to tantalize your taste buds and get you in the mood for what’s to come.Main Courses Our main courses are the stars of the show, featuring time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Indulge in our Smoked Brisket, slow-cooked to perfection and served with a tangy barbecue sauce. Or savor the comfort of our Buttermilk Fried Chicken, crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, accompanied by homemade biscuits and creamy gravy.For seafood lovers, our Blackened Catfish is a must-try, seasoned with a blend of spices and served with collard greens and cornbread. Vegetarian options include our hearty Southern Vegetable Plate, featuring seasonal veggies and a side of our famous mac and cheese.Sides No Southern meal is complete without our selection of classic sides. Choose from creamy Coleslaw, buttery Cornbread, and savory Green Beans cooked with smoked ham hocks. Each side is made from scratch, using fresh ingredients to complement your meal perfectly.Desserts Finish your dining experience on a sweet note with our delectable desserts. Treat yourself to our signature Pecan Pie, rich and gooey, or enjoy a slice of our Homemade Peach Cobbler, served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Each dessert is a celebration of Southern sweetness that will leave you craving more.Beverages Pair your meal with our selection of classic Southern beverages. Sip on a refreshing Sweet Tea, or indulge in a glass of our House-made Lemonade. For those looking for something stronger, we offer a curated list of Southern-inspired cocktails and local craft beers.
